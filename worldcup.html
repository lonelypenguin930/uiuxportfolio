Lonely Penguin

As an avid soccer fan, I am as excited about the world cup as anyone. I wanted to contribute to the fans community by creating a player database tracking goals, assists, and a score I assign myself that evaluate a player's performance. Users can create their own teams on the web app and see how strong their squad is.

Empathize

"Databases have always existed, but I wanted create my own and share it with others."

The journey started with my own passion. My friends and I often get together to watch games and talk about soccer. I wanted to create an fun way for them to interact with my interpretation of the world cup. I talked to them and my intramural teammates, and it seems like something worth the time and effort.

Ideation

Generic database with a personal touch

The vision is simple: a database with key stats that allow users to create a squad out of a pool of players. I handpicked a number of players to populate the database, and based on my own judgement, I assigned them with a fantasy score that reflects their recent performance.

Personas

I feel like I know my personas personally

Based on my interviews, I was able to extract two personas, Energetic Johnny and Travelling Eric. By filling out the "thinks, feels, says, and does" boxes, the personas become a lot more concrete and relatable.

Storyboard

With some creativity, I turned the interview responses into a user journey

By talking to "Traveling Eric," I was able to relate to his journey of using the scooters. I then turned the journey into a storyboard that illustrates the process. I want the story to both capture the specifics of Eric's personal journey, but also representative enough to capture similar users like Eric.

Key Takeaway

The design thinking process starts with empathizing, and this project is centered around that. Through taking to real people on the streets, I was able to learn how to craft and ask objective questions, questions that offer insights and minimizes biases. This lesson will come in handy in my own design and entrepreneurial journeys.
