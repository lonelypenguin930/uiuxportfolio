Welcome to my Portfolio! I am a interaction designer obsessed with design thinking, and I am equally obsessed with minimalistic design!

Projects

UnBox - a P2P marketplace to reduce campus waste

UnBox is a non-profit start-up I built to help students find an easy and fast solution to deal with their items during the move-out phase at the end of the school year. My team and I went through the whole design-thinking process to make the project happen.

SPIN Journey Mapping - A dive into personas

SPIN is a user journey map I created to explore how users interact with the public scooter interfaces. It is an attempt to look into the "empathize" stage of design thinking to understand the users.

World Cup Database - Frontend Programming

I created this World Cup database to track the statistics of key players. It is a way to combine design with implementation that creates value for soccer fans.

Deall! - An Iterative Redesign Journey

We took a deep dive into designing an interface for a job search platform. We followed the process of design-thinking, from prototyping to testing and iterating.
